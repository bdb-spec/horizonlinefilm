The Story

Years ago, a tragedy on Christmas Eve trapped the Spirit of Christmas in young Cole Martin's mind. Now an adult, Cole is haunted by guilt and the spirit's insistent plans. When a teenage girl resembling his lost sister posts about her holiday blues, the spirit sees a perfect opportunity for redemption. As Cole stumbles through a wild comedy-thriller, he must navigate his sanity, the ancient spirit's schemes, and the whims of a capricious teenager.

A film by Bennet De Brabandere
