The Story

Set in a post-apocalyptic world where the human population has been wiped out by a fungus, Danny searches for his daughter. Ten years later, alone, he heads north. After breaking his leg, he is rescued by an orphaned girl, Sam. Together they wait out the winter while his leg mends. In the spring, Danny discovers a boot-print that does not belong to him — another man is alive. The newcomer challenges Danny's version of the apocalypse, threatening his new life. The inevitable clash reveals that true power is not rooted in the apocalyptic past, but with who will create the future.
