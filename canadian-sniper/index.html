The Story

A former elite sniper returns to his small Canadian hometown after military service, struggling with PTSD while investigating his father's disappearance. As reality and traumatic memories blur, he battles violent impulses while attempting to rebuild his life. Shot in Levack, Greater Sudbury, the film is a quiet, haunting study of a man after war — exploring trauma through posture, routine, and silence rather than overt spectacle.

Written & directed by Michel Kandinsky
